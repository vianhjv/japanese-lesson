Subject: Regarding the Rules for After-Hours Work (Overtime)

To all employees of ABC Design,

For the health and work-life balance of our employees, our company basically does not encourage overtime. However, when necessary, such as before a project deadline, you may work overtime. In such cases, please be sure to follow the rules below.

First, before working overtime, you must obtain permission from your supervisor. You must not work overtime without permission.

Second, for safety and security, you must not remain in the office after 10 PM. The last person to leave the office must turn off the air conditioning and lights, and lock the office door.

Third, you must not take company computers or important documents outside the company. Company information is a valuable asset.

Additionally, for overtime of less than 30 minutes, it is not necessary to submit an overtime request form. However, for anything longer, please file a request properly according to the regulations.

Thank you for your cooperation.

General Affairs Department
